Nordic Conference on Development Economics

About

This annual conference serves as a meeting point for development economists active in the Nordic countries, but is also targeted more generally to academic researchers in Europe and beyond.

The conference was established in 2002, led by Magnus Hatlebakk and Finn Tarp. Since its inception, the conference has rotated among universities in Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden. Each edition features keynote lectures by internationally renowned scholars, and provides a platform for both established and early-career researchers to share new empirical and theoretical work that can inform development policy.
